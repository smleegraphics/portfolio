These character studies were created as warm-up exercises and style explorations. Each sketch took between 30 minutes to 2 hours.

## Process

Most of these were drawn in Procreate on iPad, using a limited brush set to maintain consistency. The focus was on capturing personality and expression rather than detailed rendering.

## Notes

Character design is an area I'm actively working to improve. These studies help me explore different facial structures, expressions, and stylization levels.
