In my first character and environment model, I took inspiration from my favorite winter moment : curling up with a good book and a warm cup of tea on a cold, snowy night. I also wanted to create a stylized character that would fit into the old fashioned reading nook. I loved the exaggerated features of Anton Ego from Ratatouille. So I created Frank, Anton's softer, domesticated brother, whose wardrobe consists entirely of sweater vests and corduroy pants.
