This project is a 3D recreation of Honey Lemon’s bedroom from Big Hero 6, inspired by artist Dinah Kalaha. I built the environment in Unreal Engine and created props in Maya, with materials textured in Substance Painter. 

<div class="mt-6"></div>

The goal was to learn the full environment art pipeline — from modeling and material creation, to lighting, scene composition, and set dressing — bringing the space to life with attention to detail and atmosphere.
